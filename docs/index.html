Everything a tutor does

Built for real conversation practice at your JLPT level, N5 → N1

💬Free chat & voice calls

Type or just talk — real-time spoken conversation with automatic turn-taking.

🎬36 roleplay scenarios

Order ramen, survive a job interview, check into a ryokan, report a lost wallet at the kōban…

✏️Corrections that teach

Every message checked, mistakes explained in English and remembered. Use an advanced kanji correctly? You get praised, not corrected.

📚Offline dictionary

JMdict, 490k+ entries. Tap any word in any chat for furigana, meaning, and an example.

🃏Spaced repetition

Anki-style reviews of words you save — each grade button shows exactly when the word comes back.

📱Works on your phone

Your computer does the AI work; your phone is the screen. Free secure link via Tailscale, installs like an app.

Running in 3 steps

One-time setup, ~10 minutes. The wizard picks an AI model that fits your hardware.

1

Install Ollama — the free local AI engine Kaiwa uses by default.

2

Download Kaiwa above, unzip, and run:

macOS:    ./setup.sh  then  ./run.sh
Windows:  .\setup.ps1  then  .\run.ps1
3

Open http://localhost:8130 — the onboarding wizard takes it from there. こんにちは！
