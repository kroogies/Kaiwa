会  Everything in one quiet app

A whole language partner, not just a chatbot

Practise the way you actually will in Japan — then let Kaiwa quietly remember every slip and turn it into review.

Free chat

Natural conversation tuned to your JLPT level, N5 through N1. It meets you where you are and gently stretches you.

Voice calls

Just talk. Real-time spoken back-and-forth with automatic mic hand-off — no typing, no buttons to fumble.

36 roleplay scenes

Ramen shop, job interview, izakaya, ryokan, kōban… or write your own scene and cast Kaiwa in any role.

Gentle corrections

Every message is checked in the background. Mistakes are explained in English — and good advanced kanji is praised, not "fixed".

Furigana & romaji

Toggle readings, romaji and translation per conversation. Tap any word for an instant offline dictionary meaning.

490k-word dictionary

The full JMdict, searchable in Japanese or English — completely offline, instant, and never confabulated.

Spaced repetition

Anki-style SRS built in. Words you stumble on return exactly when you're about to forget them.

It remembers you

Recent mistakes and vocabulary feed back into the tutor's memory, so lessons compound instead of resetting.

Progress & reports

Streaks, minutes practised, mistake patterns, and per-session reports you can save as PDF.

選  Your AI, your rules

Private by default. Powerful by choice.

Out of the box Kaiwa runs a free local model on your own hardware — nothing is sent anywhere. Want more horsepower? Plug in your own cloud key and only that provider ever sees your text. You always hold the trade-off.
